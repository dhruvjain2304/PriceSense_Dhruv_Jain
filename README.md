# PriceSense Analytics Report  
### Strategic Pricing Intelligence, Demand Elasticity & Channel Performance

A comprehensive business analytics and pricing intelligence project focused on understanding consumer demand, pricing elasticity, channel performance, and premium product positioning across a health & wellness consumer goods portfolio.

---

## Project Overview

This project analyzes **48,444 verified transactions** across multiple product categories, customer personas, channels, city tiers, and pricing buckets to uncover actionable strategic recommendations for revenue growth.

The analysis focuses on:

- Revenue optimization
- Premium pricing opportunities
- Price elasticity analysis
- Consumer segmentation
- Channel efficiency benchmarking
- Geographic pricing intelligence
- Occasion-based demand behavior
- Competitor pricing benchmarking

---

## Business Problem

The portfolio exhibited:

- Heavy dependence on low-price products
- Under-monetized premium buyers
- Channel efficiency gaps
- Pricing inconsistencies across city tiers
- Weak premium SKU migration
- Data quality fragmentation

The objective was to identify:

- High-margin pricing opportunities
- Revenue expansion levers
- Premium positioning strategy
- Consumer willingness-to-pay patterns
- Operational and data quality improvements

## Key Strategic Insights

### Premium SKU Opportunity
The ₹100–150 price bucket generated exceptionally high revenue-per-order despite low order share, indicating a strong premium monetization opportunity.

### Persona Misalignment
Budget persona buyers were spending more on premium products than Premium personas, revealing segmentation inefficiencies.

### Channel Efficiency Gap
The App channel significantly outperformed Retail in revenue-per-order, indicating stronger premium conversion in digital ecosystems.

### Tier-Based Pricing Potential
Tier 2 cities demonstrated the highest average price acceptance, highlighting untapped premium pricing opportunities.

### Occasion-Driven Revenue
Late-night and Religious Fasting occasions generated disproportionately high revenue, suggesting strong occasion-led merchandising potential.

---

## Strategic Recommendations

### Premium SKU Migration
Shift users from low-value price buckets toward mid-premium and premium SKUs through:
- Bundling
- Checkout upsells
- Loyalty incentives
- Premium collections

### Persona-Based Pricing Redesign
Align persona segmentation with actual willingness-to-pay patterns.

### Tier-Differentiated Pricing
Implement geography-specific pricing strategies for Tier 1, 2, and 3 markets.

### Occasion-Based Collections
Launch curated product collections targeting:
- Late-night consumption
- Religious fasting
- Fitness routines
- On-the-go usage

### Premium Channel Optimization
Position:
- App & Gym Kiosk → Premium channels
- Website & Retail → Volume channels

### Data Quality Remediation
Fix:
- Duplicate category naming
- Geographic inconsistencies
- Missing tier classifications
- Metadata normalization
